## 🔍 Overview

<a href="#ampliTube5">AmpliTube 5</a> is IK Multimedia's flagship guitar and bass amp modeling platform for Mac — offering over 400 modeled pieces of gear in a fully configurable signal chain that replicates the experience of building a real guitar rig, from the pedalboard to the amplifier head to the speaker cabinet and studio microphone placement.

The <a href="#ampliTube5">amplifier head and cabinet simulation</a> models the physical and electrical behavior of tube amplifiers and speaker enclosures at the circuit level — capturing harmonic saturation, dynamic response, and the frequency shaping of different amp-and-cabinet pairings that define the characteristic sounds of electric guitar across genres. The library spans American clean tones, British crunch, vintage blues overdrive, and modern high-gain metal amplification, covering the full range of electric guitar and bass styles that professional players work across. The <a href="#ampliTube5">microphone and room placement</a> completes the virtual recording chain — selecting from different virtual microphone types, positioning them at different angles and distances from the cabinet face, and blending room ambience that gives the recorded tone the spatial quality of a studio miking session without needing a physical room or real microphone.

The <a href="#ampliTube5">flexible signal chain editor</a> constructs the rig in any configuration — effects pedals placed before the amp input, in the effects loop between pre and power amp stages, or after the cabinet in the rack section. Multiple amplifier heads, cabinet combinations, and microphone placements build complex routing scenarios that go beyond what a standard single-amp rig provides. The <a href="#ampliTube5">gear library breadth</a> covers 400-plus officially modeled pieces from recognized amplifier and effects manufacturers across decades of guitar gear history.

<a href="#ampliTube5">TONEX neural amp capture integration</a> loads AI-trained models of real amplifiers directly into AmpliTube 5 — TONEX Captures are machine-learning models trained on specific real hardware, bringing the exact character of captured amplifiers into the AmpliTube signal chain as additional amp models. <a href="#ampliTube5">Built-in multitrack recorder</a> captures guitar performances directly within AmpliTube 5 — laying down multiple takes, building arrangement demos, and recording complete guitar parts without launching a separate DAW.

<a href="#ampliTube5">Looper and chromatic tuner</a> support practice and performance workflows alongside tone shaping — the looper records and layers phrases for practice and composition, and the tuner keeps instruments in tune without leaving the application. <a href="#ampliTube5">AU and VST plugin operation</a> inserts AmpliTube 5 directly into Logic Pro, GarageBand, Ableton Live, and other compatible hosts for seamless studio recording integration.

## ❓ FAQ

<details>
<summary>What is AmpliTube 5?</summary>

AmpliTube 5 is IK Multimedia's professional guitar and bass amp modeling application for Mac, providing 400-plus modeled pieces of gear in a configurable signal chain editor alongside TONEX neural amp capture integration.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How many gear models are included?</summary>

400-plus officially modeled pieces including amplifier heads, speaker cabinets, microphones, stompbox effects, and rack processors. Additional gear is available through the CustomShop.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does AmpliTube 5 work as a plugin in Logic Pro?</summary>

Yes. AmpliTube 5 operates as an Audio Units plugin in Logic Pro, GarageBand, and other AU-compatible Mac DAWs, as well as VST in compatible hosts.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is TONEX and how does it work with AmpliTube 5?</summary>

TONEX is IK Multimedia's AI amp capture platform. TONEX Captures are machine-learning models trained on specific real amplifiers. These captures load into AmpliTube 5 as additional amp models alongside the traditionally modeled gear.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does AmpliTube 5 have a looper?</summary>

Yes. A built-in looper records and layers phrases for practice and performance alongside the amp modeling environment.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What latency can I expect for real-time guitar playing?</summary>

AmpliTube 5 is designed for sub-10ms operation with compatible audio interfaces. Actual latency depends on the audio interface and buffer settings.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can AmpliTube 5 record audio?</summary>

Yes. A built-in multitrack recorder captures guitar performances directly within AmpliTube 5 without a separate DAW.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Is there a free version of AmpliTube?</summary>

Yes. AmpliTube CS is a free version with a starter set of gear models. Additional models purchase individually through the CustomShop or in bundles.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does AmpliTube 5 run on Apple Silicon?</summary>

Yes. Current versions support Apple Silicon Macs with native performance.

</details>
